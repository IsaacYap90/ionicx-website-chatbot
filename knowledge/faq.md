# IonicX FAQ

## What is IonicX?
IonicX is a Singapore-based AI technology company that builds AI-powered websites, chatbots, and digital solutions for businesses. We help companies automate customer engagement, generate leads, and grow revenue using cutting-edge AI.

## How long does it take to build an AI website?
Typical timelines:
- **Starter package:** 2-3 weeks
- **Growth package:** 3-5 weeks
- **Enterprise package:** 6-10 weeks

## What's the process?
1. **Discovery Call** – We understand your business, goals, and requirements (30 min, free)
2. **Proposal & Agreement** – We send a detailed proposal with scope, timeline, and pricing
3. **Design & Development** – Our team builds your AI-powered solution
4. **Review & Feedback** – You review and we iterate until you're happy
5. **Launch & Training** – We deploy and train your team on using the platform
6. **Ongoing Support** – We provide maintenance and support post-launch

## Do I need technical knowledge?
No! Our solutions are designed for business owners. We handle all the technical work. You'll get a simple dashboard to manage content and view analytics.

## What industries do you serve?
We work with businesses across all industries including:
- Professional services (law firms, accounting, consulting)
- Healthcare and wellness
- Education and training
- E-commerce and retail
- F&B and hospitality
- Real estate
- Financial services

## What languages do you support?
Our AI solutions support multiple languages including English, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Malay, Tamil, and more. The AI chatbot can converse naturally in these languages.

## Do you provide ongoing support?
Yes, all packages include post-launch support. We also offer monthly maintenance plans for continuous improvements, updates, and AI model fine-tuning.
